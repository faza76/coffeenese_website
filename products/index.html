Indonesian
Origin

This coffee was grown in Garut, West Java, Indonesia, where the high altitudes and unique terroir create a distinct flavor profile.

  • Altitude — 1100 - 1400 meters above sea level, This coffee was grown at high altitude, which allows the beans to develop slowly and results in a more complex and nuanced flavor.

  • Processing — Natural, Honey, Washed and Semi-Washed.

  • Varietas — Sigararuntang, Bourbon.

  • Cupping Notes — Nutty aroma, bright acidity, medium body, chocolate finish..

  • Bean Size — 16-18 screen size, the beans in this batch are all between 16 and 18 screen size, which means they are large and uniform in size. This can help ensure even roasting and consistent flavor.
